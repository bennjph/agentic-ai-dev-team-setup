### Detailed Real-World Scenarios (49 KB)

**4 Comprehensive Case Studies:**

#### Scenario 1: Healthcare Patient Triage Chatbot
- **Domain:** Life-critical healthcare  
- **Scale:** 500k patients/year, 5 hospital locations  
- **Outcome:** $3.2M annual savings, 22% ER visit reduction  
- **Key Learning:** Safety-first evaluation, HIPAA compliance, <1hr hotfix capability

#### Scenario 2: E-Commerce Product Recommendation Engine
- **Domain:** Revenue-driven recommendations  
- **Scale:** 500k monthly users, 50k products  
- **Outcome:** 35% conversion lift, $4.2M annual revenue increase  
- **Key Learning:** Offline metrics don't predict online performance, A/B test integration

#### Scenario 3: Legal Document Contract Review AI
- **Domain:** High-stakes legal liability  
- **Scale:** 120 attorneys, corporate contracts  
- **Outcome:** 42% time savings, 99.1% recall on risky clauses  
- **Key Learning:** Recall > precision, transparency about AI limitations

#### Scenario 4: Global Customer Support Chatbot (Multi-Lingual)
- **Domain:** Multi-cultural support  
- **Scale:** 50k customers, 8 languages  
- **Outcome:** 43% ticket deflection, $4.68M annual savings  
- **Key Learning:** Cultural nuance > direct translation, regional variants matter

**Each scenario includes:** Business context, team structure, decision points, issues discovered, 12-month outcomes, lessons learned
